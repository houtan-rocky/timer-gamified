DESCRIPTION

Timer Gamified is a powerful, feature-rich productivity timer application that transforms your work sessions into an engaging, gamified experience. Built with a focus on motivation and accountability, this desktop app helps you stay focused, track your progress, and celebrate your achievements.

Whether you're using the Pomodoro Technique, time-blocking your day, or simply need a reliable timer for focused work sessions, Timer Gamified provides all the tools you need. The app features customizable timer presets, visual and audio feedback, progress tracking, and a unique win/loss system that gamifies your productivity.

Key highlights include an always-on-top overlay window for constant visibility, customizable zone-based alerts (OK, Critical, Danger), automatic progress tracking with beautiful heatmap visualizations, and celebration effects when you complete your sessions. All data is stored locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy and offline functionality.

Perfect for students, professionals, freelancers, and anyone looking to improve their time management and productivity through gamification.

PRODUCT FEATURES

1. Customizable Timer Presets - Create and manage multiple timer presets with different durations for various tasks

2. Gamification System - Track wins and losses for each timer session, turning productivity into a game

3. Always-On-Top Overlay Window - Keep the timer visible at all times with a compact, floating overlay window

4. Zone-Based Feedback - Visual and audio alerts for OK, Critical, and Danger zones based on remaining time percentage

5. Customizable Celebration Effects - Confetti animations and custom sounds when you successfully complete a timer session

6. Activity Heatmap - Beautiful year-view heatmap showing your daily productivity patterns and win/loss history

7. Custom Sound Uploads - Upload and use your own sound files for timer alerts, celebrations, and zone notifications

8. Custom Image Uploads - Personalize your overlay window with custom background images

9. Desktop Notifications - System notifications for zone changes and timer completion

10. Progress Bar Integration - Visual progress indicator in the Windows taskbar showing timer completion status

11. Auto-Messages - Set up custom messages that appear at specific time percentages during your timer

12. Multiple Sound Options - Choose from built-in sounds (beep, heartbeat, battle win, epic success) or use custom sounds

13. End-of-Timer Questions - Optional "Win?" prompt to track whether you successfully completed your focused session

14. Statistics Tracking - Daily win/loss counts and historical activity data

15. Dark Theme - Beautiful dark interface with customizable accent colors

16. Timer Synchronization - Main window and overlay window stay perfectly synchronized

17. Customizable Zone Thresholds - Adjust Critical and Danger zone percentages to match your preferences

18. Mute Controls - Global mute toggle and per-zone sound controls

19. License System - Secure local license key storage for app activation

20. Privacy-First Design - All data stored locally, no internet connection required, no data collection
